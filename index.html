Liancheng Fang Ph.D. Student in Computer Science
Publications

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Chicago. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Philip S. Yu. My research interest is in generative models for structured data, with a recent focus on Diffusion Language Models.

Previously, I received my M.S. in Biomedical Data Science and Mathematics from University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I am grateful to have worked with Prof. Yin Li and Prof. Vikas Singh. I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Sun Yat-sen University.

I am currently an applied scientist intern at Amazon. I was a intern at Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence.
